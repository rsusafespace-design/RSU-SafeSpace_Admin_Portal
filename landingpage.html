Platform Features

Designed to support student mental wellness and counseling efficiency

Appointment Scheduling

Students can easily schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments with counselors based on real-time availability.

Secure Messaging

Confidential chat and video call system for private communication between students and counselors.

Self-Assessment Tools

Informational questionnaires to help students gauge their current well-being and track progress over time.

Confidential Reporting

Secure reporting system for bullying, harassment, or sensitive concerns with immediate counselor notification.

Resource Library

Curated collection of mental health resources, articles, and self-help materials accessible anytime.

Progress Tracking

Counselors can monitor student progress, session history, and maintain organized records efficiently.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles behind the RSU Safespace platform

Our Mission

To develop a secure, user-friendly web-based platform that enhances the accessibility, efficiency, and effectiveness of the Guidance Counseling Office (GCSO) at Romblon State University. We aim to provide students with confidential access to mental health support while empowering counselors with tools to manage services effectively.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital mental health support system within RSU, creating a stigma-free environment where every student feels safe, heard, and supported in their mental wellness journey. We envision a campus community where seeking help is normalized and accessible to all.

About RSU Safespace

Empowering students and counselors through digital guidance and mental wellness support.

What is RSU Safespace?

RSU Safespace is a web-based platform developed by BSIT students of Romblon State University to enhance the Guidance Counseling Service Office (GCSO) through digital innovation. The platform provides students with confidential, accessible, and efficient mental health support services, while empowering counselors with tools for appointment management, secure messaging, and progress tracking.

Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a stigma-free environment where every student feels safe, heard, and supported in their mental wellness journey. RSU Safespace is designed to make seeking help easy, private, and effective for all members of the RSU community.
